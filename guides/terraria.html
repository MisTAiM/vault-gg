TERRARIA

Boss data curated for VAULT.GG. Difficulty ratings relative to first encounter. Click bosses on wiki for full strategies.
King Slime
⚔ 1/10
Pre-HM
Spawn with Slime Crown. Jump to avoid, kill slimes it spawns. Drops Slime Hook.
Eye of Cthulhu
⚔ 2/10
Pre-HM
Phase 1: dodge charges. Phase 2: avoid Servants of Cthulhu. Kill at night (it despawns at dawn).
Eater of Worlds
⚔ 3/10
Corruption
Break into segments. Vile Powder on Crimson blocks. Drops Shadow Scales for armor.
Brain of Cthulhu
⚔ 3/10
Crimson
Kill Creepers first, then Brain teleports. Drops Tissue Samples for armor.
Queen Bee
⚔ 3/10
Jungle
Optional but worth it. Bee armor, Bee's Knees bow, Hive Pack. Break Larva in hive.
Skeletron
⚔ 4/10
Pre-HM
Kill hands first, then head. Only at night. Head spins phase 2 — stay above it.
Wall of Flesh
⚔ 5/10
Underworld
HARDMODE UNLOCK. Throw Guide Voodoo Doll into lava. Run left the entire fight.
Skeletron Prime
⚔ 7/10
Hardmode
Most dangerous mechanical boss. Kill saw arm and cannon arm first for safety.
The Twins
⚔ 7/10
Hardmode
Kill Retinazer before Spazmatism. Flamethrower phase 2 is very dangerous.
The Destroyer
⚔ 6/10
Hardmode
Easiest mech boss. Skybridge + piercing weapon. Drops Hallowed Bars.
Plantera
⚔ 8/10
Post-Mech
Only in Jungle after all 3 Mech bosses. Break bulb to spawn. Stay close.
Moon Lord
⚔ 10/10
End Game
Final boss. 3 eyes + tongue. Phantasmal Deathray oneshots. Stay mobile. Nebula armor recommended.
